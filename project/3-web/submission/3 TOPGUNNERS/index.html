We make different food with upgrades

Malaysian cuisine is known for its diverse and flavorful dishes, influenced by a blend of Malay, Chinese, Indian, and indigenous cultures. Here are some popular Malaysian foods you should try: all. the. time.
Nasi Lemak: Considered the national dish, nasi lemak is fragrant coconut rice served with sambal (spicy chili paste), fried anchovies, peanuts, sliced cucumbers, and a hard-boiled egg. It's often accompanied by various side dishes like fried chicken, beef rendang, or squid sambal. Satay: Grilled skewers of marinated meat (usually chicken, beef, or lamb) served with a peanut sauce and cucumber slices. Satay is a favorite street food and is commonly enjoyed as a snack or appetizer. Roti Canai: A popular Indian-influenced flatbread served with a side of curry. It's crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, perfect for dipping in the flavorful curry sauce.

FAQ

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of the food you want to order, give us a quick phone call. We’ll ask you a few questions about the type of cuisine you desire, the number of servings needed, any specific dietary requirements, etc. Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place and come up with a firm quote for your catering needs.

+

How long it takes to complete the cook

The preparation time for any food order is normally dictated by the client. If you have a specific timeframe in mind, we will attempt to accommodate it for you. The main general delay in fulfilling a food order is waiting for the necessary ingredients and details to be provided by the client for the preparation of the dishes.

+

How do I associate with the food Company?

The process of preparing your food order begins when you contact us with your requirements. We analyze your preferences and respond to you. Based on further discussions, you can choose a menu that suits you the best. After that, we begin the process of food preparation and cooking.

+

Can you help my current site look more professional?

Yes. Give us your requirements and we have experienced expertise to help you give a new tastiest food that really wonders!

+

When do I pay?

For most food orders, equal payments are made at the time of placing the order, upon the midway point of the preparation, and upon delivery or completion of the order. However, we can work with you to set up a payment schedule that meets your needs. We understand that ordering food can be a significant expense, and we want to help you budget for it in whatever way possible. We accept payments through various methods, including checks, PayPal, and all major credit cards.
